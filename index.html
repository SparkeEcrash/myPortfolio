i am passionately curious

All my life I've always been studying or working in different countries. I've come to view programs similarily to the way I view people. They both have different languages, different ways of doing the same things, and different quirks that when exploited become incredible power. The years I have spent as a developer has made me believe that despite cultural differences people are actually very much the same. The more I am able to understand the intricate and harmonious flow of technological systems, the more I am able to understand people better and use these insights to become a better person. Becoming a better developer is a bonus.
